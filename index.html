A Looming Terror

The topic of student safety is always on the rise. With every passing minute, the defense against strict gun laws becomes weaker, unfortunately with the loss of more innocent children.

Just last year alone, there were 38 school shootings that resulted in a total of 42 people injured and 21 dead.

That number adds to the previous 120 school shootings that took place between 2018-2022, and gives us a catestrophic 158 shootings, that could have been prevented.

As of April 18th, there have already been 14 school shootings across the U.S, resulting in 9 deaths and 25 injuries.

We need to prioritize this issue before we're too late.
